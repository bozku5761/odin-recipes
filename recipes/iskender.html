Iskender Kebap

Live picture of an Iskender kebap

  • 4 pita bread rounds
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - chopped
  • 2 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can tomato puree
  • ground cumin to taste
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Arrange pita bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly toasted. Cut pita bread into bite-sized pieces; keep warm.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in chicken, onion, and garlic; cook until chicken juices run clear. Mix in tomato purée; season with cumin, salt, and pepper. Continue cooking for 10 minutes.
  5. Arrange toasted pita pieces in a serving dish. Drizzle with butter and top with chicken mixture. Garnish with yogurt and parsley to serve.
